AIResearch

Transforming academic research through artificial intelligence

About The Platform

AIResearch is an intelligent research companion designed to help students, researchers, and professionals understand complex academic papers faster. Users can upload research papers, ask questions about the content, and receive AI-generated explanations with relevant citations. The platform combines natural language processing and semantic search to make research more accessible and efficient.

What The Website Can Do

Research Paper Analysis

Upload academic papers and allow the AI system to process, understand, and analyze complex research documents.

Intelligent Question Answering

Ask questions about your uploaded paper and receive context-aware answers based only on the provided research.

Semantic Search

Our AI finds the most relevant sections of a paper instead of relying on simple keyword matching.

AI Summaries

Convert lengthy academic papers into clear explanations that are easier to understand and review.

Citation Generation

Automatically generate citation information to help organize and reference important research.

Research History

Save previous conversations and revisit important insights from your research sessions.
